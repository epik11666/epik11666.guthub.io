About Me

I am a level designer with hands-on experience across AAA and mobile level production, working in Unity and Unreal Engine on everything from large open-world areas to tight hyper-casual loops.

My approach centers on player-first thinking — every map, encounter, and system I design is evaluated through the lens of how it makes the player feel. Tension, pacing, atmosphere, and flow are not afterthoughts; they are the design.

I work fluently across level design, GDD authoring, engine integration, and cross-team collaboration — comfortable preparing task breakdowns for programmers and artists, and driving iteration from first blockout to final polish.
